Revealing Your Safe Location

Someone discovers and reveals the address of your refuge, shelter, safe house, or new home — putting you and potentially others at risk.

What You Might Notice

  • The other person references or appears near your safe location

    They know where you're staying despite it being confidential.

  • You receive communications indicating your address is known

    Messages referencing your current location.

What You Can Do

  • Contact your support service or refuge immediately

    They can help assess the risk and arrange relocation if needed.

  • Contact police if there's an immediate safety threat

    Call 000 if you're in danger.

  • Review how the location may have been discovered

    Check devices for tracking, review social media for location clues, and consider whether anyone might have shared the information.

Important: This resource provides general information, not personal advice. Every situation is different. The actions suggested here may not be safe in your specific circumstances — particularly if the person causing harm could notice changes to your devices or accounts. Always consider your physical safety first.

If you need personalised support, contact 1800RESPECT (1800 737 732) or your local specialist domestic violence service. If you are in immediate danger, call 000.

Safe Location Disclosure

Revealing the location of victim's shelter, safe house, refuge, or hidden address to the perpetrator or others. May involve sharing information publicly, telling mutual contacts, or using technology to discover and disclose the protected location, directly endangering victim's physical safety.

ID: TFA-T-7002
Sub-techniques:  No sub-techniques
ⓘ
Tactic: Physical Enablement
Created: 04 February 2026
Last Modified: 04 February 2026

Mitigations

ID Mitigation
TFA-M-7004 Safe Location Digital Hygiene
Disable location services. Strip metadata from images. Avoid tagging safe location digitally.
TFA-M-7005 Secure Communication from Safe Location
Use new device and accounts. Only communicate location to those who must know via secure channels.

Detection Indicators

ID Detection Indicator
TFA-D-7003 Safe Location Discovery
Adversary appears near or references safe location. Immediate safety emergency.
TFA-D-7004 Photo Metadata Exposure
Shared photos contain GPS coordinates revealing safe location.
