Pretending to Be You Online

Someone creates fake accounts impersonating you — using your name, photos, and personal details to damage your reputation, harass others in your name, or create confusion.

What You Might Notice

  • People tell you about accounts or messages 'from you' that you didn't create or send

    Friends receive messages from someone pretending to be you.

  • Fake profiles using your photos and name appear on social media or dating sites

    Your identity is being used without your consent.

  • You're blamed for things the impersonator said or did

    People are angry at you for things you didn't do.

What You Can Do

  • Report impersonation accounts to the platform immediately

    All major platforms have dedicated impersonation reporting tools.

  • Notify your contacts

    Let people know about the fake accounts so they're not deceived.

  • Document the fake accounts

    Screenshot profiles, messages, and any damage caused.

  • Report to police

    Impersonation for the purpose of harassment is a criminal offence.

Important: This information is general in nature and does not constitute personal, legal, or clinical advice. Every situation is different. Actions that are safe for one person may not be safe for another — especially if the person causing harm may escalate when they notice changes. If you're unsure, speak with a specialist service before making changes to your devices or accounts.

Impersonation Harassment

Creating fake accounts impersonating victim to damage their reputation, relationships, or employment. Fake accounts may send messages to victim's contacts, post inappropriate content, engage in harmful activities, or create the appearance that victim is behaving erratically or antisocially.

ID: TFA-T-3009
Sub-techniques:  No sub-techniques
ⓘ
Tactic: Harassment & Intimidation
Created: 04 February 2026
Last Modified: 04 February 2026

Mitigations

ID Mitigation
TFA-M-3021 Impersonation Reporting
Report fake accounts to platform using impersonation reporting process.
TFA-M-3022 Network Notification
Inform contacts of fake accounts and request verification through trusted channels.

Detection Indicators

ID Detection Indicator
TFA-D-3017 Contact Reports of Impersonation Messages
Contacts report receiving messages from accounts impersonating target.
TFA-D-3018 Fake Account Discovery
Profiles using target's name, photos, or information discovered.
